Check if you can get help from Civil Legal Advice

If you’re eligible for legal aid, you may be able to get free, confidential legal advice from Civil Legal Advice (CLA). CLA can only help with problems related to:

  • debt, if your home is at risk
  • housing
  • education
  • family
  • domestic violence
  • discrimination
  • benefit appeals

Check if you qualify financially for help from Civil Legal Advice

If you qualify financially, you can ask to speak to a Civil Legal Advice operator about your problem at the end of this service.

Before you start

To use this service, you’ll need:

  • last month’s payslips (if you’re working)
  • last month’s bank statements
  • details of any benefits you got last month

Legal aid is different in Scotland and Northern Ireland.

If you’ve been charged with a crime, this service doesn’t apply to you. Ask your solicitor about criminal legal aid.

This service is only for adults. If you’re under 18, talk to a solicitor about legal aid.
