You can only use this service for non-criminal (civil) problems. If you have been charged with a crime, talk to your solicitor about criminal legal aid.

Before you start

To use this service, you’ll need:

  • last month’s payslips (if you’re working)
  • last month’s bank statements
  • details of any benefits you got last month

Start

If you might be able to get legal aid, you can speak to an operator about your problem at the end of this service.

What is legal aid?

  • it can help you cover the cost of legal advice
  • it can get someone to speak for you in court
  • it can help you resolve family problems without going to court

Who can get legal aid?

You (and your partner if you have one) must meet BOTH of the following conditions:

  • your income should not be more than £2,657 a month before tax
  • your savings should not be more than £8,000
