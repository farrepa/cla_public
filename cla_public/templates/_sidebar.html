Who can get legal aid?

To qualify for legal aid, you must have a problem that is covered by legal aid and meet these conditions:

  • your income (or combined income if you have a partner) should not be more than £2,657 a month before tax
  • your savings (or combined savings if you have a partner) should not be more than £8,000
  • you (and your partner if you have one) should have no more than £733 a month left after we've taken certain living expenses into account
